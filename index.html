About About

At IHN, we believe that effective legal representation is built on trust, integrity, and unwavering commitment to our clients. Our team consists of skilled attorneys with extensive experience across diverse legal fields, including corporate law, family law, criminal defense. Whether navigating complex business transactions or defending personal rights, we bring a thorough, strategic approach to every case.

Founded in 1998 by Andreiev Yurii, IHN began with a clear mission: to offer personalized, high-quality legal representation to individuals and businesses in need. What started as a small practice dedicated to small business advocacy has since grown into a multi-disciplinary firm with a reputation for excellence and integrity. Over the years, IHN has grown into a trusted name known for its client-focused approach to results.
